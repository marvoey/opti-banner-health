# Web Content Management Platform Requirements

## Background

To streamline content management and distribution across multiple digital properties, the organization requires a centralized, scalable, and flexible Web Content Management (WCM) system that enables seamless content sharing, governance, and optimization. The WCM must support multi-site management, global content updates, scheduling, and user access controls while maintaining a unified design system and ensuring compliance with accessibility and SEO best practices.

**This WCM platform will serve as a foundation for digital experience optimization, ensuring a cohesive and scalable content strategy that enhances user engagement and operational efficiency across all digital touchpoints.**

## 1. General Requirements

- **The solution must support low code, no code visual-first website building.**

- **Must enable collaborative workflows for marketing, design, and development teams.**

- **Should be backed by enterprise-grade security, scalability, and compliance standards.**

## 2. Design & Content Management

- Support for drag-and-drop page building with reusable, designer-approved components.

- Support for integrated design systems.

- Ability to scale design systems across multiple sites using shared libraries.

- Offer native A/B testing and personalization tools.

- Provide visual and programmatic content editing via headless APIs.

- A single source of truth for content that can be shared across internal and third-party platforms, with global update functionality and site-specific overrides.

- Integrated content and site search, knowledge base support, and voice search functionality for enhanced discoverability.

- AI-driven content classification, SEO recommendations, and indexing to improve content efficiency and personalization.

- Custom form creation with tracking capabilities, as well as robust file hosting and media management.

## 3. Collaboration & Workflow

- Must include page branching to allow multiple designers to work in parallel.

- Support for custom user roles and permissions to control access and publishing rights.

- Include publishing workflows with preview and approval capabilities as well as automated version control.

- Provide a Site Activity Log and Audit Log API for monitoring and compliance.

## 4. Performance & Hosting

- Must offer enterprise-level uptime SLAs and fast page loads via a CDN.

- Capable of handling large traffic surges with reliable traffic management.

- Support end-to-end localization for multi-lingual audiences.

## 5. Analytics & Optimization

- Provide tools for conversion optimization, including A/B testing and personalization.

- Adobe Analytics integration, content tagging, and third-party script management to track engagement and optimize content performance.

## 6. Security & Compliance

- Must support Single Sign-On (SSO) and SCIM for identity management.

- Comply with SOC 2 Type II standards.

- Include advanced DDoS protection (e.g., AWS Shield).

- Provide detailed documentation of security audit compliance and risk frameworks.

- Include web accessibility tools for WCAG standards.

## 7. Support & Services

- Must offer dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM).

- Include onboarding, training, and technical consulting services.

- Provide on-demand phone support and access to a resource hub.

## 8. Integrations & Ecosystem

- Must integrate with essential marketing tools: Marketo, Adobe Experience Cloud.
